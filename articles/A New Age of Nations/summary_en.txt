The article discusses the implications of artificial intelligence (AI) on national competitive advantage, emphasizing that success in the AI era will depend more on societal integration and the ability to manage AI's effects than on technological mastery alone. It identifies key societal characteristics essential for competitiveness and warns that AI could either strengthen or destabilize these foundations, ultimately shaping the future of nations in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. The analysis calls for U.S. policymakers to adopt a broader perspective on societal factors to maintain national advantage in the AI era.
